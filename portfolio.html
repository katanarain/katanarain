DIGITAL HOLOGRAPHIC MICROSCOPE
[May 2022 - present]

ABOUT DEVICE

Worked on a device that will be deployed in the soil to constantly monitor the movement of bacteria, archaea, fungi and protozoa distribution. This way we can study their spatial patterns and interactions within the microbiome at a micrometer scale.

MY ROLE

Designed and built the housing for the device in order to keep the electronics organized and safe from water damage while deployed in the soil. Worked on the optical fluidics system that allows water in and out through specifically engineered channels for optimal flow.

"ATTACK ON TITAN" YO-YO
[February - May 2022]

ABOUT YO-YO

Created a yo-yo based on a popular anime called "Attack on Titan". It comprises of three injection molded parts and a thermoformed "Titan" face. The yo-yo depicts one of the Titans breaking through a wall.

MY ROLE

The process involved creating aluminum block molds using a CNC Machine and then injection molding with plastic. The yo-yo components were kept together using a press fit design on outer ring. Titan face hand-drawn in Photoshop.

SKILLS

Design for Manufacture
CNC Machining
Injection molding
Thermo-forming
CAD/CAM
Illustrator

TEAM

Worked on a small team of MIT Mechanical Engineering Undergraduates. Manufactured over 50 identical yo-yos with a drop tested press fit design!
